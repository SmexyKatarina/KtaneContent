Dammit, why do Mafia and Werewolf have to be taken.

On the module, there are eight lettered dots, representing people. There are also arrows between each person, representing who they visited that night. Based on their position on their module, find who each person on the module is and do the right things that are necessary.

This table tells you each person’s “role”. For each person on the module, look at the letter on the dot and the correct column. Use the first column if you are checking someone on the top row. Use the second column if you are checking someone on the bottom row. Use the third column if you are checking someone on the right side. Use the fourth column otherwise.

When you are ready, click on the moon.

Click on any person to submit their answers. Upon clicking, a menu will open up. You will have to click their role, and then send all appropriate night status “messages” based on what happened to them that night, in the order they are given in the list below.

Messages include:

  1. “You were jailed!”
  2. “You were attacked and healed by a doctor!”
  3. “You were attacked and protected by a bodyguard!”
  4. “You were attacked and healed by a Guardian Angel!”
  5. “You were attacked but your defense was too strong!” (For roles that can survive attacks on their own)
  6. “You were role-blocked!” (For escorts and consorts) or “Your target was in jail!”
  7. “You were converted by a vampire!”
  8. “You are dead!”

Most roles also have their own role-specific messages. Refer to the appendix for those. Repeat this process to any 3 people in order to solve the module.

Appendix RO735

Here is a comprehensive list of the roles and what they do. The roles are listed in alphabetical order.

The highest priority abilities take place first, then the second highest, etc. If multiple roles have equal priority, assume that they happen at the same time. The highest priority is 1, the second highest is 2, etc.

Ambusher

  • If anybody visits the person that the Ambusher targeted, the Ambusher will do a Basic attack to the highest priority person who visited them.
  • The Ambusher has a priority of 3.
  • The Ambusher has no role-specific messages.

Arsonist

  • The Arsonist douses someone with gasoline (which does nothing).
  • The Arsonist has a priority of 5, and can survive all Basic attacks.
  • The Arsonist has no role-specific messages.

Bodyguard

  • If the target of the Bodyguard gets attacked, the target will be given Basic and Powerful attack immunity, and the Bodyguard will do a Basic attack to whoever tried to attack them, and will die in the process.
  • The Bodyguard has a priority of 3.
  • Bodyguards cannot stop Unstoppable attacks.
  • The Bodyguard has no role-specific messages.

Bulletproof

  • The Bulletproof does nothing to their target, and can survive all Basic and Powerful attacks.
  • The Bulletproof has no role-specific messages.

Consigliere

  • The Consigliere will know what their target’s role is.
  • The Consigliere has a priority of 4.
  • They will get the message “Your target’s role is [role].”

Consort

  • Anybody the consort visits will not be able to perform their ability if their priority is lower than that of the Consort.
  • The Consort has a priority of 2.
  • If anybody attempts to roleblock the Consort, the Consort will receive the message, “Someone tried to roleblock you and failed.”

Sheriff

  • The Sheriff will check whoever they visit. Ambushers, Consorts, Framers, Hypnotists, Mafiosos, Serial Killers, Vampires, Werewolves, and Witches are considered evil. All other roles are not evil.
  • The Sheriff has a priority of 4.
  • If the Sheriff’s target is evil, the Sheriff will receive the message, “Your target is evil!” Otherwise, the Sheriff will receive the message, “Your target is good!”

Disguiser

  • The Disguiser will appear as their target’s role to all other roles.
  • The Disguiser has a priority of 3.
  • The Disguiser has no role-specific messages.

Doctor

  • If the Doctor’s target is attacked, they will get Basic attack immunity and their target will get the message “You were attacked and healed by a Doctor!”
  • The Doctor has a priority of 2.
  • The Doctor cannot stop Powerful and Unstoppable attacks.
  • The Doctor has no role-specific messages.

Escort

  • The Escort is the same as the Consort.

Framer

  • If the Framer’s target is visited by a Cop, they will appear evil to the cop.
  • The Framer has a priority of 3.
  • The Framer has no role-specific messages.

Godfather

  • If there are no Mafioso, the Godfather will do a Basic attack on their target.
  • If there is a Mafioso, the Mafioso will do a Basic attack on whoever the Godfather targeted.
  • The Godfather has a priority of 5, and can survive Basic attacks.
  • The Godfather has no role-specific messages.

Guardian Angel

  • The Guardian Angel’s target will be immune to any and all death.
  • The Guardian Angel has a priority is 3.
  • The Guardian Angel has no role-specific messages.

Hypnotist

  • The Hypnotist’s target will get a random fake message. Use the last digit of the serial number to determine which message they get from the list on Page 2.
  • The Hypnotist has a priority of 3.
  • The Hypnotist has no role-specific messages.

Jailor

  • The Jailor roleblocks their target, overriding immunity to roleblocks.
  • The Jailor protects their target from Basic and Powerful attacks.
  • The Jailor does not visit their target to perform their action.
  • The Jailor has a priority of 1.
  • The Jailor cannot stop unstoppable attacks.
  • The Jailor has no role-specific messages.
  • If someone tries to target the Jailor’s target, they will fail.

Jester

  • The Jester still visits people, but does nothing to their target.
  • The Jester has a priority of 6.
  • The Jester has no role-specific messages.

Lookout

  • The Lookout will get a list of people who visited their target last night.
  • The Lookout has a priority of 3.

Mafioso

  • If there is no Godfather, the Mafioso will do a Basic attack on their target.
  • If there is a Godfather, the Mafioso will do a Basic attack on the Godfather’s target.
  • The Mafioso has a priority of 5.
  • The Mafioso has no role-specific messages.

Serial Killer

  • The Serial Killer will do a Basic attack to their target.
  • If they are role-blocked, they will do a Basic attack to everyone who role-blocked them.

Spy

  • The Spy will bug somebody, and will get all of the non-role-specific messages that their target got.
  • The Spy has a priority of 6.
  • The Spy has no role-specific messages.

Tracker

  • The Tracker will follow somebody and see who they visited.
  • They will get the message “Your target visited [who they visited]”.
  • The Tracker has a priority of 4.

Vampire

  • The Vampire will convert their target to a vampire.
  • This doesn’t actually do anything other than give them the message “You were converted by a Vampire!”
  • If the Vampire tries to convert a Vampire Hunter, the Vampire will get attacked with a Basic attack.
  • The Vampire has a priority of 5.
  • The Vampire has no role-specific messages.

Vampire Hunter

  • If the Vampire Hunter’s target is a Vampire, the Vampire Hunter will do a Basic attack to them.
  • The Vampire Hunter has a priority of 5.
  • The Vampire Hunter has no role-specific messages.

Veteran

  • The Veteran ignores their arrow and does not visit anyone.
  • If anyone visits the Veteran, they will get attacked by a Basic attack.
  • The Veteran has no priority because they don’t move.
  • The Veteran has no role-specific messages.

Vigilante

  • The Vigilante will do a Basic attack to whoever they visit.
  • The Vigilante has a priority of 5.
  • The Vigilante has no role-specific messages.

Werewolf

  • The Werewolf will do a Powerful attack to your target and anyone who visits them.
  • The Werewolf has a priority of 5, and can survive a Basic attack.
  • The Werewolf has no role-specific messages.
