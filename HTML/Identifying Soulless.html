On the Subject of Identifying Soulless

So, imagine the nerves- imagine the nerves, FCing past that.

This module contains a keyboard, a text box, the word "SOULLESS", and a display with the logo for Soulless 5.

To begin a stage, press the display. The module will then play part of a section from one of the six Soulless tracks.

Enter the exact name of the module into the text box using the keyboard (all words are capitalized), then press the enter key to submit your answer.

If your answer was correct, one of the lights to the side of the display will light up. Solve three stages to disarm the module.

If you did not answer correctly, the module will strike and reset.

Pressing any key while an animation or section is playing will skip the animation and the music. However, the module will always wait for ten seconds when you start a section, meaning skipping it does not save time.
